May 2026 · Announcement

GameDay Helpers Is Coming to Tampa Bay

Every youth coach knows the moment. It's first pitch, the parents are settling into their chairs, and you still need someone to run GameChanger. So you scan the bleachers and start asking. Again.

GameDay Helpers exists to end that scramble. We connect Tampa Bay coaches with vetted local helpers who keep score, run GameChanger, work the scoreboard, and run the livestream, so coaches can coach and parents can actually watch their kid play.

We're launching in Tampa's District 6 little leagues, from Bayshore to West Tampa, in summer 2026. Right now we're signing up our founding helpers and coaches. If that's you, get in early.

May 2026 · For Helpers

Get Paid to Do Something You Already Know

If you can follow a baseball or softball game and tap through an app, you can be a GameDay Helper. Parents, teens 14 and up, and retirees who love the game all make great helpers.

The deal is simple: you pick the little leagues you'll travel to, we match you to games there, you show up and keep score, and the coach pays you directly. You set your availability, and there's no commitment until you claim a game.

Never used GameChanger? Not a problem. It's easy to learn and we'll point you to a quick walkthrough before your first game. Founding helpers get first pick of games when the season starts.

May 2026 · For Coaches

One Season Pass, A Scorekeeper All Year

We built our coach plan to be dead simple and cheap: $99 flat for the whole year. That's it. No per-game fees to us, no surprises.

Your season pass gets you access to our network of vetted helpers for every game on your schedule. Need someone Saturday? Request a helper and we line one up from your league. You pay your helper directly for the game, and that game pay never touches us, so every dollar reaches the person doing the work.

The pass covers the part that actually eats your time: finding and confirming a reliable scorekeeper. We handle that all season so you can keep your head in the game.

May 2026 · How It Works

Why We Don't Touch the Money

We made a deliberate choice early on: GameDay Helpers does not sit in the middle of the payment between a coach and a helper. The coach pays the helper directly, every time.

Why? Two reasons. First, it keeps every dollar of game pay going to the helper instead of getting skimmed. Second, it keeps the whole thing simple and trustworthy, especially since some of our helpers are teenagers. We broker the match, not the money.

What we charge for is the part with real value: finding, vetting, and assigning a reliable helper so a coach never has to beg the bleachers again. That's the $99 season pass. The game pay is between the coach and the helper, exactly where it should be.
