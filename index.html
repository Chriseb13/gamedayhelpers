How it works

Here's how it works.

01
HELPERS SIGN UP
Local helpers apply and get vetted before they ever work a game.
02
A COACH NEEDS A HAND
A coach has a game and nobody to run GameChanger or keep score.
03
WE MATCH THEM
We connect the coach with a vetted helper who works that little league.
04
COACH PAYS THE HELPER
After the game, the coach pays the helper directly at the rate they set. We take no cut.
05
BOTH LEAVE A REVIEW
Coach and helper rate each other, so the most reliable people rise to the top.
